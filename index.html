Meet Atlas.io

An AI that learns your business and runs beside you.

Atlas.io is built into every Atlas Rental.io dashboard. Tell it what you rent — cars, homes, apartments, yachts, gear, anything — and it scopes and builds the perfect dashboard for you: the right KPIs, labels, booking flow and pricing. Then it keeps working beside you: adjusting settings, answering questions, drafting messages, and flagging what needs attention. Whoever you are, whatever you rent, it fits how you work.

  • "Set my weekend rates 15% higher" — done, across the fleet.
  • Spots idle assets and suggests promotions to fill them.
  • Explains your analytics in plain English, on demand.
